I am Aryakumar Mishra

An engineering student currently exploring Renewable Energy Management (REM) as part of my semester coursework. This portfolio represents my journey through the fascinating world of sustainable energy solutions, from understanding solar photovoltaic systems to analyzing wind energy potential and exploring energy storage technologies.

Through this REM course, I have delved deep into various aspects of renewable energy including resource assessment, system design, economic analysis, and policy frameworks. Each assignment, case study, and research project has strengthened my understanding of how we can transition toward a cleaner, more sustainable energy future.

This website serves as a comprehensive showcase of my academic work and learning progress in the field of renewable energy management.

What I Study

Key topics covered in my Renewable Energy Management coursework

Solar Energy Systems

Photovoltaic technology, solar thermal systems, resource assessment, and grid integration of solar power.

Wind Energy

Wind turbine technology, site assessment, aerodynamics, and offshore wind farm development.

Bioenergy

Biomass conversion technologies, biogas production, biofuels, and waste-to-energy systems.

Hydropower

Small and large hydro systems, pumped storage, run-of-river plants, and environmental considerations.

Energy Storage

Battery technologies, pumped hydro storage, thermal storage, and emerging storage solutions.

Smart Grid & Integration

Grid modernization, demand response, distributed generation, and renewable energy forecasting.
